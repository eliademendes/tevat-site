About the Project

Tevat is a platform that encourages people to share ideas to improve quality of life, with a focus on environmental and infrastructure issues. The best ideas are rewarded with TEVAT tokens.

TEVAT Token

The TEVAT token is the foundation of our ecosystem. It rewards participants and helps fund sustainable and transformative ideas.

Roadmap

  • Q2 2025: Token and website launch
  • Q3 2025: Idea platform and DAO
  • Q4 2025: Listing on DEXs
  • 2026: Partnership expansion

Governance

TEVAT holders will be able to vote on proposals to define the future of the platform.
